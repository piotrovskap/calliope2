## Observations (for the rebrand IA)

- Top-level nav is overloaded (Platform · GRC · Industries · Solutions · Deployment ·
  Security · About · Team · Careers · Pricing · Blog · Contact · Sign up · Download).
- Product surface is fragmented (6 tools + 9 "core" pages) with no parent narrative.
- Rebrand collapses this into **Product (Build/Run/Govern) · Solutions · Pricing ·
  Resources** with one primary CTA — see the new landing's mega-menu.
