FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What are the 19 integrated tools?

One platform bundles the full stack — AI Lab (notebooks), AI IDE, Chat Studio, Deep Agent, Langflow, secure browser, AGTerm, DB Loadr, File Manager, model hosting, data agents, datasets, prompt engineering and more — all under one login and one bill.

Are AI tokens included in the base price?

No. You bring your own AI model keys (BYOK) with zero markup on tokens. For managed deployments, AI token packs are available as a pay-as-you-go add-on.

What does BYOC mean?

Bring Your Own Cloud. Calliope AI runs in your own AWS account and VPC — you own the data and the bill. We provision it automatically, up and running in under an hour.

What is the minimum commitment?

A 10-user minimum on the BYOC and Managed plans, with no long-term contract. Enterprise pricing is custom.

Can I start with BYOC and move to Managed later?

Yes. You can start in your own cloud and move to a Calliope-managed deployment — or to Enterprise — at any time.

What support is included?

BYOC includes community and docs support. Managed adds priority support and a dedicated CSM, plus managed upgrades, monitoring and patching. Enterprise adds a custom SLA.

Do you support on-premises or air-gapped deployments?

Yes, on Enterprise — multi-region, on-prem and air-gapped deployments are supported, with security reviews and custom compliance mappings.

Who is Calliope Labs Inc?

Calliope Labs Inc is the company behind Calliope AI — based in Miami, FL and built by CONFLICT.
